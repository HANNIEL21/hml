about us

about HML

HORST Mining Limited (HML) is a leading mining company offering a range of services in the mining industry in Nigeria. We know how cumbersome the mining and quarrying process can be, that is why we have designed our services to provide support in Drilling, Blasting, Mining and Quarrying to our clients to enable them focus on their core business operations for greater efficiency and profitability. We bring to the mining/ construction industry wealth of experience, innovative applications, best practices in our offerings.

Mission Statement

To provide quality support services to intending and existing mining/quarrying companies in a safe and efficient manner by providing support services for the clients to enable them focus on their core business operations, enhancing proficiency and profitability.

Vision

To become the leading and foremost mining contractor first in Nigeria then globally. To attain highest excellence such that any company that thinks of support services for their mining projects will have Horst Mining Limited first on their mind.
